Basecamp - At a glance

Make Basecamp's Home screen more dynamic.

Launch prototype View the code

What's been happening? What‚Äôs next? Those are the two questions I have when I open Basecamp. Today's Home screen doesn't answer them right away. I click into projects. Scan the Activity page. What if the answers were right there, on the cards, at a glance?

Why not a full redesign? Basecamp's Home is warm. Inviting. Most other products feel like data-tables on steroids. Cold and sterile. That's not Basecamp. I didn't want to lose that.

So I focused on the cards. What if they were just a little more dynamic? Helpful, not busy. Enough to answer those two questions. Customizable, with a click.

Whether you're working on one project or many, I think you're going to like it.
